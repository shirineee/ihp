Raising Conscious Kids

An intimate community conversation with Dr. Shefali Tsabary — clinical psychologist, New York Times bestselling author, and Oprah's "favorite parenting expert."

Presented by Shirin Aryanpour & Geno La Placa · Illuminate Her Path × Teen (CO)Lab

Date October 7, 2026 Wednesday
Time TBD Announcing soon
Location TBD Marin County, CA
Price TBD Details coming soon

Dr. Shefali Tsabary has spent her career helping parents show up differently for their kids — and for themselves. In September 2026, she launches two new books, Raising Conscious Daughters and Raising Conscious Sons, and we're bringing that conversation directly to our community.

A rare chance to hear directly from one of the most trusted voices in conscious parenting.

Youth mental health has become one of the quiet, urgent challenges of our time. Nationally, 40% of high school students report persistent feelings of sadness or hopelessness. In California, 94% of young people ages 14–25 report regular mental health challenges — a number that keeps climbing. Here in Marin, that pressure often hides behind achievement and privilege, rarely spoken about out loud.

This gathering is an invitation to bring that conversation into the open — with real tools, honest science, and a community of parents and educators ready to show up differently.

Shirin Aryanpour Founder, Illuminate Her Path

Shirin Aryanpour is the founder of Illuminate Her Path, a platform devoted to empowering women through education, wellness, and authentic community. With 16+ years as a physical therapist and a mother herself, she has long been drawn to the connection between our own healing and the way we show up for the next generation. Her humanitarian work co-founding the Northern California chapter of Operation Smile, and her journey as a two-time breast cancer thriver, have deepened her belief that raising conscious kids starts with parents doing their own inner work first.

As co-host of Raising Conscious Kids, Shirin brings her passion for presence, resilience, and community to this conversation with Dr. Shefali Tsabary — creating space for parents and educators in Marin to explore what it really means to raise emotionally healthy, connected children in an anxious world.

Dr. Shefali Tsabary Clinical Psychologist & New York Times Bestselling Author

Dr. Shefali Tsabary is a clinical psychologist and New York Times bestselling author widely known as Oprah's "favorite parenting expert." Her work has reshaped how a generation of parents think about raising children — moving away from control and perfectionism, toward presence and genuine connection. In September 2026, she launches two new books, Raising Conscious Daughters and Raising Conscious Sons, continuing her mission to help families build resilience, emotional literacy, and deeper connection at home.

Geno La Placa Executive Leadership & Life Coach, Speaker & Retreat Facilitator

Geno La Placa is an Executive Leadership and Life Coach, speaker, and retreat facilitator who helps high-achieving women lead, parent, and live with deeper presence and purpose. A former hedge fund CFO, she now blends strategy with soul — certified in Dr. Shefali's Conscious Parenting Method™, Dr. Gabor Maté's Compassionate Inquiry, and trained in neuroscience, somatic psychology, and trauma-informed facilitation.

Geno specializes in emotional and intuitive leadership, empowered parenting, and the mother-daughter relationship. At Raising Conscious Kids, she co-hosts this conversation alongside Shirin, bringing her own depth of experience guiding parents toward more conscious, connected relationships with their children.

Co-hosted by Shirin Aryanpour of Illuminate Her Path and Geno La Placa of Teen (CO)Lab.
