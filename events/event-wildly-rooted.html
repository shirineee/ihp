Wildly Rooted

Wednesday, September 30, 2026 · The Willow Camp, Stinson Beach

Register Now
Date Sept 30, 2026 Wednesday
Time 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM Full schedule to follow
Location The Willow Camp 3325 Hwy 1, Stinson Beach, CA — Get Directions
Capacity 50 Guests Intentionally small & intimate

A day of movement in nature, nourishing food, sound healing, sacred circles, and meaningful conversation. Intentionally curated for women to come home to themselves.

Not because we need fixing. But because every woman deserves the opportunity to come home to herself.

Whether you're navigating a new season of life, longing for deeper connection, or simply craving a day to pause, breathe, and reconnect… this gathering is for you.

As you read this, if a woman comes to mind — a sister, a friend, a colleague, or a neighbor who would be nourished by a day like this — I hope you'll invite her.

My dream is to gather women of every generation and every walk of life into one beautiful circle where we remember that healing grows in community, belonging, and shared experience. I would be deeply honored to have you there.

What to Expect

A day held with intention.

Nervous System Regulation

Gentle, intentional movement to reconnect with your body.

Nourishing Food

Thoughtfully prepared Grazing Table with seasonal healing foods meant to nourish both the body and spirit.

Sound Healing

A guided sound journey to settle the nervous system.

Sacred Circles

Held space for honest and meaningful conversation and deep connection with women across generations and walks of life.

A Breathtaking Setting

Held at The Willow Camp, steps from Stinson Beach.

Full run-of-show and what to wear/bring will be shared closer to the gathering.

Guided By

Wisdom Keepers

Shirin Aryanpour Founder, Illuminate Her Path

Healing isn't a destination. It's a practice — found in how we nourish our bodies, care for our spirits, move with intention, and surround ourselves with women who remind us that we were never meant to do life alone. That journey became the inspiration behind Illuminate Her Path, and I'm so excited to finally invite you into something that has been on my heart for a long time. My dream is to gather women of every generation and every walk of life into one beautiful circle — where we remember that healing grows in community, belonging, and shared experience. I would be deeply honored to have you there.

— Shirin, Founder of Illuminate Her Path

Liya Garber Kundalini Activation & Manifestation Leader

Liya Garber is a Kundalini Activation & Manifestation Leader and E-RYT 500 yoga and meditation teacher known for her joyful presence and deep commitment to women's empowerment. Based in the Bay Area, she is the founder of Aura Kundalini Yoga, blending Kundalini energy work with modern mind-reprogramming practices. After stepping away from a demanding corporate career, Liya turned to Kundalini Yoga and holistic healing — a transformation that restored her health and reconnected her to her true path.

At Wildly Rooted, Liya will guide participants through kundalini-inspired movement and manifestation practice, creating a safe, uplifting space to expand consciousness and step into your fullest potential.

Mana Shams Wisdom Keeper, Spiritual Guide & Mystic Dancer

Mana Shams is a wisdom keeper, spiritual guide, mystic dancer, and space holder devoted to helping people return home to themselves. Trained in Intuition Medicine©, bioenergetic healing, and bioresonance sound therapy, she blends ancient wisdom with a modern understanding of the body to help others access their intuition and nurture emotional, physical, and spiritual well-being.

At Wildly Rooted, Mana will guide participants through grounding practices, gratitude, and immersive sound healing — offering a sanctuary where the nervous system can soften, the heart can open, and deep restoration can begin.

Tiffany Rowe Facilitator, Coach & Restorative Yogi

Tiffany Rowe is a facilitator, coach, restorative yogi, and mental health advocate who leads with courage and vulnerability. Her work is grounded in lived experience — the fixing, hiding, and armoring up that so many of us know — and the discovery that real healing comes from allowing ourselves to feel our way to developing a strong and open heart and deep self-trust.

At Wildly Rooted, Tiffany brings her signature blend of restorative practices and honest, open facilitation to sacred circle — holding space for real conversation, courage, and connection.

"The bravest, kindest, most resilient way to live and lead."

In Partnership With

Our Catering Partner

Mariam Shewan Creative Director & Food Designer, Gourmet Grazing Goat

Mariam Shewan is the creative heart behind Gourmet Grazing Goat, known for transforming fresh, high-quality ingredients into stunning grazing tables and charcuterie displays. With an eye for styling, design, and hospitality, she believes beauty lives in the details — from the flowers to the final spread — and that no two tables should ever look the same. Every display she creates is built around the people it's for, designed to turn a gathering into a genuine experience.

At Wildly Rooted, Mariam and Gourmet Grazing Goat are catering the day's nourishment — a beautiful, thoughtfully crafted spread that reflects the gathering's spirit of care, honoring the day's pillar of nourishment and healing.
