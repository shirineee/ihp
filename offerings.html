Offerings

What We Offer

Gatherings, workshops, and community experiences designed to help women reconnect with themselves and each other.

Speaker & Moderator

Conversations Worth Showing Up For

Shirin brings warmth, presence, and thoughtful facilitation to panels, fireside chats, and moderated conversations — creating space for real dialogue around women's health, wellness, and personal growth. Whether hosting or guiding the conversation, every appearance is rooted in authenticity and connection.

Wellness & Retreats

Soulful Gatherings, Intentionally Curated

From single-day soulful gatherings to fully immersive retreats, Illuminate Her Path designs wellness experiences that blend movement, nourishment, and community. Every retreat is intentionally curated to help women pause, reconnect, and return to themselves.

Sacred Circles

Held Space for Honest Connection

Sacred circles are intimate, guided spaces for honest conversation, reflection, and connection. Held with care and held space, these gatherings remind us that healing grows best in community — witnessed, supported, and never alone.

Book Launches

Bringing Big Ideas Into the Room

Illuminate Her Path partners with authors and thought leaders to bring transformative books to life through intimate community conversations. From author talks to book launch celebrations, these gatherings turn a book's message into a shared, lived experience.
