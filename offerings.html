Speaker & Moderator

Invite Shirin to Speak

Shirin brings warmth, presence, and thoughtful facilitation to panels, fireside chats, and moderated conversations. For your conference, gathering, or community event, she creates space for real dialogue around women's health, wellness, and personal growth — every appearance rooted in authenticity and connection.

Wellness & Retreats

Co-Create a Retreat With Shirin

From single-day soulful gatherings to fully immersive retreats, Shirin designs wellness experiences that blend movement, nourishment, and community. For your own community or circle, every retreat is intentionally curated to help attendees pause, reconnect, and return to themselves.

Sacred Circles

Welcome Shirin Into Your Circle

Sacred circles are intimate, guided spaces for honest conversation, reflection, and connection. Shirin can hold space for your group or community — a reminder that healing grows best when it's witnessed, supported, and shared rather than carried alone.

Book Launches

Partner With Shirin on Your Launch

Shirin partners with authors and thought leaders to bring transformative books to life through intimate community conversations. From an author talk to a full launch celebration, she helps turn a book's message into a shared, lived experience for the people gathered around it.
