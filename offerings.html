Speaker & Moderator

Bring Shirin to Your Stage

Shirin brings warmth, presence, and thoughtful facilitation to panels, fireside chats, and moderated conversations. Whether you're planning a conference, corporate event, or community gathering, she creates space for real dialogue around women's health, wellness, and personal growth — every appearance rooted in authenticity and connection.

Wellness & Retreats

Hire Shirin to Design Your Retreat

From single-day soulful gatherings to fully immersive retreats, Shirin designs wellness experiences that blend movement, nourishment, and community. Whether for your company, community, or private group, every retreat is intentionally curated to help attendees pause, reconnect, and return to themselves.

Sacred Circles

Invite Shirin to Hold Space

Sacred circles are intimate, guided spaces for honest conversation, reflection, and connection. Book Shirin to hold space for your group, team, or community — a reminder that healing grows best when it's witnessed, supported, and shared rather than carried alone.

Book Launches

Shirin partners with authors and thought leaders to bring transformative books to life through intimate community conversations. From hosting an author talk to designing a full launch celebration, she helps turn a book's message into a shared, lived experience for your audience.
