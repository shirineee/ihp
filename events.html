Every part of this day has been intentionally curated — from the breathtaking setting to the incredible facilitators, movement, nourishing food, sound healing, sacred circles, and meaningful conversations. My hope is that every woman leaves feeling more grounded, more connected, and more like herself.

Not because we need fixing. But because every woman deserves the opportunity to come home to herself.

Whether you're navigating a new season of life, longing for deeper connection, or simply craving a day to pause, breathe, and reconnect… this gathering is for you.

As you read this, if a woman comes to mind — a sister, a friend, a colleague, or a neighbor who would be nourished by a day like this — I hope you'll invite her.

My dream is to gather women of every generation and every walk of life into one beautiful circle where we remember that healing grows in community, belonging, and shared experience. I would be deeply honored to have you there.

Facilitators & Guides

Shirin Aryanpour Founder, Illuminate Her Path

Shirin Aryanpour is the visionary founder of Illuminate Her Path, an impact-driven platform and digital community devoted to empowering women through education, wellness, connection, and authentic sisterhood. With 16+ years of experience as a physical therapist, she has supported people of all ages, from athletes to elders, on their journeys toward strength and wholeness. Her global humanitarian work includes co-founding the Northern California chapter of Operation Smile, helping deliver free surgical care to children with cleft conditions for over a decade. A two-time breast cancer thriver, Shirin's own healing inspired her deeper mission: to champion women's health, equity, and empowerment. Through IHP, she curates transformational gatherings, spiritual workshops, and community-centered events that blend wellness, energy work, and soulful connection. She believes that when women rise together, they illuminate the world, one heart at a time.
