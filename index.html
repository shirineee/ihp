Why Illuminate Her Path Exists

A journey that became an invitation.

Healing isn't a destination. It's a practice — found in how we nourish our bodies, care for our spirits, move with intention, and surround ourselves with women who remind us that we were never meant to do life alone.

That journey became the inspiration behind Illuminate Her Path, and I'm so excited to finally invite you into something that has been on my heart for a long time.

My dream is to gather women of every generation and every walk of life into one beautiful circle — where we remember that healing grows in community, belonging, and shared experience.

I would be deeply honored to have you there.

— Shirin, Founder of Illuminate Her Path
