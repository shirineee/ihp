Why Illuminate Her Path Exists

Meet the Founder

Shirin Aryanpour is the visionary founder of Illuminate Her Path, an impact-driven platform and digital community devoted to empowering women through education, wellness, connection, and authentic sisterhood.

With 16+ years of experience as a physical therapist, she has supported people of all ages, from athletes to elders, on their journeys toward strength and wholeness. Her global humanitarian work includes co-founding the Northern California chapter of Operation Smile, helping deliver free surgical care to children with cleft conditions for over a decade.

A two-time breast cancer thriver, Shirin's own healing inspired her deeper mission: to champion women's health, equity, and empowerment. Through IHP, she curates transformational gatherings, spiritual workshops, and community-centered events that blend wellness, energy work, and soulful connection.

She believes that when women rise together, they illuminate the world, one heart at a time.
